The Hong Kong Tourism Board (HKTB) has launched the Anti-epidemic Hygiene Measures Certification Scheme in partnership with the Hong Kong Quality Assurance Agency (HKQAA). The scheme is aimed to raise awareness of epidemic prevention and provide unified guidelines on hygiene measures for shopping malls, restaurants, retail outlets, hotels, attractions, inbound tour operators and other tourism-related sectors, to ensure visitors can travel with confidence.
